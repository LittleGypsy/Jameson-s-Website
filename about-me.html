Jameson Wentworth

is a Chicago-based singer and actor, specializing in theatrical and commercial work. He grew up in small-town Indiana and continues to be a passionate fan of the Colts and Pacers (and Peyton Manning, wherever he may go).

After stepping into the roles of Aladdin and Tigger while working as a cast member in Disney World, he moved to Chicago to pursue a performance career full-time. He now lives with his girlfriend, Katharine, in the northside neighborhood of Lincoln Square. He hopes to soon add a puppy named Peyton to their family.
